  • Cathedral opening hours

    The Cathedral is open every day for private prayer and visitation.  Note that these times may vary in the event of a funeral, a wedding, or other liturgical celebrations. 

    Monday to Friday    10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
    Saturday   8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
    Sunday   9:30 AM - 6:00 PM

  • SUNDAY PARKING at the CATHEDRAL

    Parking at the cathedral is free of charge for Sunday Masses (Saturdays at 5 pm, Sundays at 10 am, 12 pm, and 5 pm) for ½ hour prior to the mass until ½ hour after mass.
